ABOUT
WHO I AM
👾

I am a game designer who thrives on planning and detailing game experiences. My primary focus is designing immersive worlds and refining gameplay elements to ensure an engaging player journey.

I specialise in systems design, monetisation analysis, and conceptual development — understanding not just how to build a game, but why players keep coming back.

While I have experience with scripting for prototyping, my expertise lies in designing gameplay flow, mechanics, and player experience rather than programming. My goal is to create games that are well-balanced, visually compelling, and strategically engaging.

📰 THE WASTED TIMES
Unity 6 · Blind-Typing Comedy · itch.io

A solo-developed comedic typing game, shipped end-to-end on itch.io. Players type letters as they appear — without knowing what they're spelling — and when the timer stops, the game reveals the sentence and roasts the player for it. Three difficulty levels, customisable timer, ragtime soundtrack, and a 1920s newspaper aesthetic.

Solo development — concept, design, code, art, audio, publish
Designed the blind-input → reveal → roast loop and three-tier difficulty system
Authored the itch.io page and v1.2 devlog end-to-end
